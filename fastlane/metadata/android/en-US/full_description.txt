Bunk Safe is an open-source, privacy-first companion app designed specifically for students to effortlessly track their attendance and Continuous Internal Evaluation (CIE) marks. 

Built with speed and local processing in mind, the app securely syncs with your college portal entirely in the background, ensuring your credentials and data never leave your device. 

Key Features:
- Real-time attendance tracking with visual safe/at-risk indicators.
- Detailed class history showing exactly which sessions you attended or missed.
- Comprehensive CIE marks breakdown with progression bars.
- Secure, local-only scraping—no external servers or telemetry.
- Beautiful, dark-themed, and responsive interface.

Never miss a safe attendance threshold again with Bunk Safe!
